## How I Approach Problems

1. **Understand the constraints**: What are the non-functional requirements? Who are the users?
2. **Design the architecture**: Where does data flow? What are the critical paths?
3. **Implement deliberately**: Each decision is intentional‚Äînaming, structure, error handling
4. **Test rigorously**: Edge cases reveal design flaws
5. **Think operationally**: Will this work at 3 AM when something breaks?
